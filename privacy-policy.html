Privacy Policy

At Prithvi Buildtech Construction, we are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when engaging with our website and services.

By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. If you have any concerns, feel free to contact us for further clarification.

1. Information We Collect

We collect data to ensure efficient service delivery and to improve your experience. This includes:

  • Personal Information – Name, contact details, company details, and project preferences.
  • Payment Information – Securely processed transaction details when making payments.
  • Website Usage Data – IP addresses, browser type, interaction history, and device information for analytics and security purposes.
  • Project Specifications – Data related to construction requirements, material preferences, and custom requests.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for business operations, including:

  • Managing project consultations and construction agreements.
  • Processing payments and invoices securely.
  • Providing customer support and project updates.
  • Enhancing website functionality and analyzing trends.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and safety regulations.

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for service provision or legal compliance:

  • Active customer and project records are stored securely.
  • Financial transactions are retained per tax and legal regulations.
  • Archived project data is anonymized for analysis and service improvement.
  • You may request deletion of your personal information unless legally required to retain it.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update inaccurate details in our records.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request deletion of your data where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@Prithvi Buildtech.com

5. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data storage and secure servers.
  • Limited access to sensitive data for authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security audits and updates to prevent breaches.

While we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your data, no system is entirely immune to security risks. We recommend that users also take precautions to protect their personal information.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to enhance functionality and analyze visitor behavior. These technologies help us:

  • Personalize your browsing experience.
  • Measure and improve website performance.
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and enhance security.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Services

We may collaborate with third-party service providers for payments, analytics, or marketing. These partners adhere to strict data protection policies, ensuring your information remains secure.

However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites linked from our platform.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any modifications will be posted on this page, and where necessary, we will notify users of significant updates.

We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
